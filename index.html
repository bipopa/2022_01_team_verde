What is ConnectNplay

ConnectNplay is a platform created to help content creator to have better interactions with its community. These platform let the creator manage the community events such as tournaments, giveaways, online meetings and online gaming. It is a great tool to have when you want to collaborate with other creators or with your community.

Time Tracking

Track the time you've spent with your contributors or creator. manage how much time do you want to spent with each individual and sort by time played. You'll get to know better your community and give the opportunity for everyone to play with you.

Community interactions

Create and grow your community here at ConnectNplay whith a series of features that are design to help the creator to have constant interaction with his contributors. Join a community of others creators and support them with a contribution.

Tournaments and giveaways

Organize and follow your tournaments an easy and interactive interface that lets you choose the users that are participating. Use the giveaways function to randomly pick someone from your community. Participate and track other's communities givesway and win lots of prizes.
